## Register

product

## Users

Thai employees at a small-to-medium company. Their primary interface is LINE chat: they check in/out, log expenses, request leave, and query reports by sending messages in a LINE group or personal chat. The web dashboard is a secondary surface, visited when they need detailed reports, monthly summaries, or admin tasks. They use the app during the workday on both mobile and desktop, often glancing quickly between LINE and the dashboard.

## Product Purpose

A personal productivity and workplace management hub accessed via LINE Bot and a web dashboard. Core domains: expense tracking with budgets and AI-powered parsing, attendance with overtime calculation and checkout reminders, leave management synced with Thai public holidays, crypto DCA tracking on Thai exchanges, shared subscription splitting, and utility tools. Success means employees spend less time on admin and more time on real work.

## Brand Personality

Calm, efficient, reliable. The interface should feel like a trusted tool that stays out of the way. No flash, no hype. Information is clear and immediate. Every interaction should feel like it was designed by someone who respects your time.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS dashboards with hero-metric cards and gradient accents
- Crypto-native dark UIs with neon colors and twitchy animations
- Overly playful or gamified internal tools
- Anything that makes a simple task take more than two clicks

## Design Principles

1. **Respect attention.** Show the most important thing first. Everything else is secondary or hidden until needed.
2. **Thai-first, always.** Typography, spacing, and layout must work with Thai script as the primary language. Never design for English and hope Thai fits later.
3. **Calm confidence.** The interface communicates reliability through restraint, not decoration. White space, clear hierarchy, predictable interactions.
4. **LINE-native mindset.** Most users live in LINE. The web dashboard should feel like a natural extension of that experience, not a separate world they have to learn.
5. **Progressive depth.** Surface-level views are simple and scannable. Details reveal on demand, never dumped all at once.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA compliance. Ensure sufficient color contrast for Thai text at all sizes. Support both light and dark modes. Respect reduced-motion preferences. All interactive elements must be keyboard accessible.
